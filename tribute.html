A brief timeline of DFW's life:

  • 1962 - Born in Ithaca, NY
  • 1985 - Graduated from Amherst College
  • 1987 - The Broom of the System is published
  • 1991 - Began teaching at Emerson College
  • 1996 - Infinite Jest is published
  • 1997 - Received a MacArthur Fellowship
  • 2002 - Became an English Professor at Pomonoa College
  • 2008 - Died in Claremont, CA
  • 2009 - Manuscript for unfinished novel, The Pale King, is published posthumously
